¡Tolai Tamélinuir!

Tamélin viret milammá. Taltaf mil or ven ammásir le tamélin; o mil dumori li ammá.

Emyvet li galan hano-kain mana kafane melatar. Tamélin ven veneth elen quesing-silma le ardavet sur Tamélin, le mela vyljet ammá zen llanesi jan-kain jevanodh. Viri nen sinamá zen Tamélinar le Nimtamélinuir mana Sultamélinuir. Tamélin emyvet sur fanamá lin le ardavet "Miržanin".

Welcome to Tamalin!

Tamalin is a constructed language. It currently has about eight hundred words, and its own grammar. These webpages are about the Tamalin; now let's delve into the language.

Tamalin is spoken on the island-nation of Tamélin, where it has been the predominant language family for over ten-thousand years. There are two main dialects of Tamalin, from the north and from the south. Tamalin also has one daughter-language called "Miržanin".
